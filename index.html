Doctor Alvarez

Education is the foundation for any doctor. Dr. Alvarez earned both of his degress from Indiana University. Prior to graduating from there with his Doctorate in Dental Surgery in 1996, Dr. Alvarez earned his Bachelor of Science in Chemistry. So it comes as no surprise that he taught part-time at the IU School of Dentistry for 17 years.

At Alvarez Family Dentistry, we bring that commitment from top-to-bottom. All of our staff aim to remain on the cutting edge of best dental practices by continuously attending seminars around the country. Beyond that, Dr. Alvarez is a graduate of the Hornbrook Comprehensive Aesthetic Dentistry Continuum, and has attended the Dawson Academy ensuring his office employs the best contemporary esthetic procedures. The result: beautiful, functionally correct dentistry achieved by a measured balancing of new practices with the wisdom of when to use them.

Dr. Alvarez is an active volunteer dentist at the local Trinity Free Clinic. He and his wife, Veronique, have two girls, Aubrie and Alexa. The family loves to travel and is active in the community.
